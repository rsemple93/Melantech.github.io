Welcome to MELANTECH

A startup tech organization dedicated to make a difference.

Get Started

MelanTech is about introducing the world of technology and programming to people of color.

We hope to encourage people of color, of all ages, to broaden their horizons and take on the challenge. In doing so, we aim to accomplish the following things:

  • Provide opportunites to learn in-demand skills and be able to take control of their future for the better.
  • Develop critical problem solving and leadership skills in a comfortable and supportive environment.
  • Realize that by taking on this challenge, upcoming coders will build confidence and will then become builders/innovators of technology.

We want everyone who joins to gains access to the tools of success and be able to thrive. The end goal is to prepare and develop new entrepreneurs with the necessary tools so that they can become apart of the growing population of minorities in the tech world and make a difference.
